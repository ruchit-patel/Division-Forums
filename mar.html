What's New

  • CBDT clarifies that if any asset acquired from undisclosed income is sold before 1.6.2016, it is the cash in hand which is to be declared as undisclosed asset under the IDS and not the cost of acquisition of asset as increased by capital gain after giving indexation benefit.

  • The Rajya Sabha passed the Goods and Services Tax (GST) Constitutional Amendment Bill

  • MCA vide its General Circular No. 08/2016 dated 29.07.2016, relaxes payment of additional fees on filing of eforms AOC-4, AOC-4 (XBRL), AOC-4 (CFS) and MGT-7

  • Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MCA) has notified National Company Law Tribunal Rules, 2016 (NCLT) and National Company Law Appellate Tribunal Rules, 2016 (NCLAT) vide its notification dated 21st day of July 2016, which shall came into force from the date of its publication in Official Gazette.

  • ICDS implementation deferred by one year-will now be effective from Assessment Year 2017-18
